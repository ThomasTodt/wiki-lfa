# Liga de Futebol Antifascista (LFA)

Welcome to the official wiki of the **Liga de Futebol Antifascista (LFA)**. This space documents the history, teams, seasons, and competitions that define our league.

---

![LFA Logo](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/35204e0a-e169-4491-8d65-efb936430ce3)

The **Liga de Futebol Antifascista** is an independent football league built on solidarity, anti-fascist principles, and community integration.
